⚠️ Important: Forex trading and participation in the Lasfunding program involves significant financial risk. Please read this disclosure carefully before applying.

1. Nature of Forex Trading

Forex trading involves buying and selling currencies in global markets. Values fluctuate constantly due to economic, political, and market forces, which can result in substantial gains or significant losses in a very short time. Past performance is not indicative of future results.

2. Risk of Loss

  • You can lose some or all of the profits generated on a funded account.
  • Market conditions can change rapidly and without warning.
  • No trading strategy or tool guarantees consistent profitability.
  • Leverage magnifies both potential profits and potential losses.
  • Technical failures may result in unintended trades or missed opportunities.

3. The Setup Fee Risk

The ₦250,000 setup fee is non-refundable. It does not guarantee selection, a funded account, or any financial return. Only pay this fee if you can afford to do so without financial hardship.

4. Demo vs Live Trading

Results achieved on a demo account may differ significantly from a live funded account. Psychological pressure, spreads, slippage, and real market conditions are materially different from demo environments.

5. Funded Account Risks

  • Violation of risk rules may result in account revocation without compensation.
  • Losses exceeding defined drawdown limits may result in account closure.
  • Lasfunding may adjust funding levels based on performance and firm risk parameters.

6. Market & External Risks

Forex markets are influenced by factors outside any trader's control — economic data releases, central bank decisions, political events, natural disasters, and regulatory changes can all cause rapid and extreme price movements.

7. No Financial Advice

Nothing on the Lasfunding website or communicated by our team constitutes financial or investment advice. All content is for educational purposes only. Consult a qualified financial adviser before making any financial decisions.

8. Psychological Risk

Trading can be emotionally demanding. While we provide a psychology program, we are not mental health professionals. Traders experiencing significant emotional distress should seek appropriate professional support.
