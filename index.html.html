Our Story

Built in Lagos,
for African Traders

Lasfunding was founded by Nigerian traders who understood one truth: talent was never the problem. Access to capital — and real, structured support — was the problem.

We built the kind of program we always wished existed. One that doesn't just hand you a demo account and a rulebook, but actually invests in your development as a trader — mentally, technically, and financially.

Because we know that trading can be frustrating without any income — and financial pressure leads to emotional trading — we created this program to empower traders with low or no income, so they can focus on what truly matters: becoming consistent, disciplined professionals.

We're based in Lekki, Lagos Island, and proud to be building Africa's next generation of professional forex traders, one funded account at a time.
